Strategy comparison

Profit & loss
Cumulative mark-to-market PnL. Green = making money, red = losing.
Position
How many units you're holding. Positive = long, negative = short.
Order book + your fills
Green line = best bid, red = best ask. Dots = where your orders filled.
Spread over time
Gap between best ask and best bid. Wider spread = more potential profit per trade.
Volume imbalance
OBI = (bid_vol - ask_vol) / total. Above 0 = buy pressure, below 0 = sell pressure.
Bot activity
How many trades each bot made. Helps identify active market makers vs noise.
Trade log
Your most recent fills. Side, price, and quantity.
Parameter sweep
Each bar = one param combo. Sorted best to worst. Find what works.
